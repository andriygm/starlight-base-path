Guides lead a user through a specific task they want to accomplish, often with a sequence of steps.
Writing a good guide requires thinking about what your users are trying to do.

:::tip
Asides work alongside `starlight-base-path`: this one links to the
[reference example](/reference/example) with a root-relative URL, and the base
path (`/docs`) is still prepended in the rendered output.
:::
